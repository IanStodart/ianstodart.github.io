About Me

Hi there! Nice to meet you. My name is Ian Stodart and I am a Software Engineer. I graduated from Cal Poly Pomona with a B.S. in Computer Science in 2017. My interest in technology started when I was young, I got my first gaming system to go on vacation (Super Mario Bros), and ever since then, whether it was gaming or exploring the internet, it has been hard to get me off the computer (my parents can confirm). From my gameboy, I later transitioned into computer gaming, and became interested in how to make them, but I never knew how to get into it. When exploring college majors, I came across computer science because it seemed to align with what I was good at and what I enjoyed, little did I know what was ahead of me was the vast world of coding, which created many of the gadgets that I had most enjoyed throughout my life. The thought of one day being able to build something like R2D2 or my own Iron Man suit felt like a dream. However, that all just truly felt like science fiction until I went to college.

Throughout High School, when I wasn't doing school work, I dedicated most of my free time to Marching Band. It consumed my life, as I enjoyed music, but the act of performing while playing was something very bizare yet amazing to me. I became very dedicated to it and aspired to become very involved. During my junior year, I became the treasurer of the band and was as invovled as possible with helping the other leaders and organized many fundraiser events to help us get to competitions and minimize the cost for other students.

Anyway, I think that's enough about me, let's get to the good stuff.
